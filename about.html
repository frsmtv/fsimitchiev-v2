After classical double bass studies at the Conservatoire, François Simitchiev developed a strong interest in baroque and contemporary music, as well as electronic music. He was trained as a self-taught musician in sound recording, mixing and production, and embarked on a creative and experimental journey in parallel to his work as an instrumentalist.

His work is a minimalist and often contemplative approach to improvisation, work on repetitive movements, and is at the crossroads of classical music, sound art, ambient and electronic music, mixing acoustic instruments, samples, field recordings and machines, always placing the work of sound material at the heart of the process.

Versatile and curious, always looking for new collaborations, Francois Simitchiev is now attracted by composition and sound design for film, dance, theater, installations etc ... without wanting to leave the stage.
